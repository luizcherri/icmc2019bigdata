BNOC and HNOC softwares: A benchmarking tool to generate bipartite, k-partite or heterogeneous network models with overlapping communities

About

BNOC and HNOC are a tools for benchmarking weighted bipartite, k-partite and heterogeneous network with overlapping community structures. The tools can create network with balanced or unbalanced overlapping communities, heterogeneous community sizes, different intra- and inter-community edge densities, and with varying average degrees and clustering coefficients.
